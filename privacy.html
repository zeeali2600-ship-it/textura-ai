Privacy Policy – Textura AI

Last Updated:
This policy explains how Textura AI (the “App”) handles your data. We aim for minimal collection.

1. Summary

Textura AI lets you generate images from prompts using a third‑party AI image service (Ideogram) through our backend. We store the minimum possible data.

2. Data We Collect

  • Locally Stored Trials Count: Number of free generation attempts left (saved in localStorage on your device only).
  • Prompt (Transient): Sent to our backend to forward to Ideogram. Not saved permanently in our database (we currently do not run a persistence layer).
  • Generated Image URL (Ephemeral): Returned by the AI provider; may expire. We do not store the image server‑side.
  • Basic Server Logs: For reliability (timestamp + success or error). No profiling or advertising trackers.

3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal names, emails, addresses, or phone numbers unless you voluntarily send via support email.
  • No payment info – any future subscription will be handled by a trusted payment processor (e.g., Microsoft Store billing) which has its own policy.
  • No behavioral tracking (no Google Analytics / ads SDK).

4. Use of Data

  • Prompts are used only to generate the requested image.
  • Local trial count controls free usage.
  • Error logs help fix bugs, improve stability.

5. Third‑Party Services

  • Ideogram API: Processes your prompt to produce images. Their system may apply safety filters or store aggregate usage metrics. Refer to their own policy.
  • Render (Hosting): Hosts our backend server; transient request logs may include timestamp and status code.
  • GitHub Pages: Hosts the static frontend; standard access logs may be maintained by GitHub.

6. Cookies & Local Storage

We use localStorage to remember trial count. No tracking cookies.

7. Image Safety & User Responsibility

  • Do not input personal, confidential, or sensitive data in prompts.
  • Generated images are AI creations and may contain artifacts; you are responsible for how you use them.
  • Any attempt to generate disallowed content (e.g., explicit illegal material) may be blocked by the AI provider.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Prompts: Not stored long term.
  • Trial counter: You can clear it by clearing browser storage (localStorage).
  • Logs: Error/success indicators retained short term for troubleshooting, then rotated automatically by the hosting provider.

10. Security

Transport uses HTTPS. We keep no persistent user profile data, reducing attack surface. Still, no internet service can guarantee 100% security.

11. Future Changes

If we add accounts/subscriptions, we will update this policy before launch.

12. Your Rights

You may request deletion of any support email you sent us. Because we do not maintain personal databases, most rights are inherently satisfied (minimal data held).

13. Contact

Email: textura.help@gmail.com (example – replace with real support email). When contacting, do not include sensitive information.

14. Microsoft Store Compliance Notes

  • No collection of device identifiers beyond platform‑standard HTTP logs.
  • No sharing of personal data with advertisers.
  • Minimal functionality data only (prompt transit + image generation).

15. Consent

By using the App and clicking “Generate Image,” you consent to sending your prompt to the AI provider for image generation.
