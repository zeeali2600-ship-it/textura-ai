1. Introduction

Welcome to Textura AI (“we”, “our”, “the App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our AI image generation service. Our design goal is privacy by minimalism: do not gather data we don’t absolutely need.

2. Data We Collect

  • Prompt Text (Transient): Sent to our backend, forwarded to the AI provider (Ideogram) to generate an image. We do not permanently store prompts.
  • Generated Image URL (Ephemeral): Provided by the provider; often expires. We do not host or retain copies on our server.
  • Trial / Credits Counter (Local Only): Stored in your browser’s localStorage to track remaining free generations. Not sent to us.
  • Basic Technical Logs: Timestamp + success / failure indicator and possibly error text for debugging. No profiling, no advertising IDs.
No persistent user profile: We currently do not maintain a database of users, nor tie prompts to identity.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No names, addresses, phone numbers, or personal identifiers unless you voluntarily email support.
  • No payment card details (store billing handled by platform later, separate policy).
  • No advertising analytics SDKs (e.g., Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel).
  • No biometric, health, or location tracking data.

4. How We Use Data

  • Generate requested images from your prompt.
  • Maintain free trial limits (local counter).
  • Troubleshoot errors via aggregated log info.

5. Legal Basis (If Applicable – GDPR regions)

  • Contract / Service Performance: Processing your prompt to deliver an image.
  • Legitimate Interest: Lightweight logging to keep service stable and secure.
  • Consent: You opt in by submitting a prompt.

7. Cookies & Local Storage

No tracking cookies. Only a trial counter stored in localStorage.

8. AI Content & Safety

  • Provider may reject unsafe prompts (e.g., illegal content).
  • Images may contain artifacts or inaccuracies. Review before publishing.
  • You are responsible for ensuring generated content respects laws and third‑party rights.

9. Your Rights (Where Applicable)

  • Access / Information: You can view what is stored locally (trial count).
  • Deletion: Clear browser storage to remove local data. Prompt text is not stored persistently.
  • Objection / Restriction: Stop using the App; we keep minimal transient data only.

10. Data Retention

  • Prompts: Not retained beyond immediate processing.
  • Image URLs: Ephemeral; not archived by us.
  • Logs: Short‑term technical error entries, rotated automatically.

11. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect their data.

12. Security

  • Transport via HTTPS.
  • No persistent user database reduces risk surface.
  • Nevertheless, no system is 100% secure; use responsibly.

13. International Data Transfers

Depending on hosting zones, transient request data may pass through servers located in other countries solely to fulfill your request.
